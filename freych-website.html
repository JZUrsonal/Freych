The Technology

How FREYCH Works

Two powerful sterilization technologies work together in one smart cap — automatically, safely, and silently.

01 🔵

UV-C Irradiation

Deep-UV LEDs emit germicidal wavelengths that penetrate and destroy the DNA of bacteria, viruses, and protozoa — achieving medical-grade sterilization in minutes.

02 ⚡

Electrochemical Oxidation

EO technology generates free radicals that strip biofilm from your bottle's interior — eliminating the "stinky bottle" effect and hidden bacterial dead zones that sponges can't reach.

03 🛡️

Smart Safety System

Gyroscopic and cap sensors automatically cut power when the bottle is tilted or opened, preventing any accidental UV-C exposure. A BMS manages Li-ion power for smart, efficient operation.
